Frequently Asked Questions
  • What is KuberaVault?
    KuberaVault is a personal finance tracker for students to manage income, expenses, budgets, and more.
  • How do I add a new transaction?
    Go to the Transactions page and fill out the form to add a new income or expense.
  • How can I plan my monthly budget?
    Visit the Budget page and use the "Plan Monthly Budget" button to set your budget for each category.
  • Can I add my own categories?
    Yes, use the "Add Category" button on the Dashboard to create new income or expense categories.
  • Is my data secure?
    Your data is stored locally and only accessible to you. For best security, always log out after use.
  • Who can I contact for support?
    Please use the contact form on the main website or email the support team for help.
